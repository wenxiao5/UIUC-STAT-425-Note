In the sum contrast the intercept is the mean response for all treatments. The mean response for each diet is equal to the intercept + the coefficient for the diet. The coefficient for Diet 4 is \(\hat{\alpha_4}=-\sum_{i=1}^3 \hat{\alpha_i}\) since \(\sum_{i=1}^4 \alpha_i=0\).

The choice of contrast is not going to affect the global model fit, but it is going to affect the estimated \(\hat{\beta}\). You need to know which contrast is used before making conclusions about \(\beta\).
